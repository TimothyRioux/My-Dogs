"ABOUT ME"

My name is Timothy Michael Rioux. I am 55 years old and currently a sophomore at University of Rhode Island. My majors are Electrical
Engineering and German. My past career path was as a police officer in Attleboro, Massachusetts and later an industrial commercial electrician.
I am married to my best friend, my wife Shawna. We currently reside in Cranston Rhode Island. We have five children, three boys and two girls. We are also
grandparents a beautiful little girl. We have three dogs, 5-year-old Rottweiler “Lilly”, a 2-year-old German Shepherd “Chloe”. Chloe is currently my service dog. We have a new pup, a 5 month old

German Sheapherd, named "Bruno" . He is Chloe's half brother.

I am a military veteran and began serving in the Air Force in 1985 at the age of 18.
First stationed at Lackland Air Force Base, San Antonio, TX, then at Pease Air Force Base, Portsmouth, New Hampshire.

Later I had the great experience of being stationed at Spangdahlem Air Base (52nd Fighter Wing) , Spangdahlem, Rheinland-Pfaltz, Germany.

    1989
I witnessed the East German Exodus through Czechoslovakia into what was then West Germany. The "Berlin Wall" fell, then German Reunification. Later our air wing was challengewith “Operation Desert Storm”

    2002
I was deployed to Afghanistan, for Operation Enduring Freedom
