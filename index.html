About Me

My name is Timothy Michael Rioux. I am 55 years old and currently a sophomore at University of Rhode Island. My majors are Electrical Engineering and German. My past career path was as a police officer in Attleboro, Massachusetts and later an industrial commercial electrician.
I am married to my best friend, my wife Shawna. We currently reside in Cranston Rhode Island. We have five children, three boys and two girls. We are also grandparents a beautiful little girl.
We have three dogs, a 15-year-old mixed breed “Kiaya”, 5-year-old Rottweiler “Lilly” and a 2-year-old German Shepherd “Chloe”. Chloe is currently my service dog.

I am a military veteran and began serving in the Air Force in 1985 at the age of 18. First stationed at Lackland Air Force Base, San Antonio, TX, then at Pease Air Force Base, Portsmouth, New Hampshire.

Later I had the great experience of being stationed at Spangdahlem Air Base (52nd Fighter Wing) , Spangdahlem, Rheinland-Pfaltz, Germany.

  • It was 1989 I witnessed the East German Exodus through Czechoslovakia into what was then West Germany. Later the wall fell, then German Reunification. Later our air wing was challenged with “Operation Desert Storm”

  • In 2002, while a member of the Massachusetts National Guard, I was deployed to Afghanistan, for Operation Enduring Freedom
